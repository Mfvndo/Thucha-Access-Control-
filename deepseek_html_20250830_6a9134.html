Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security solutions

How long does installation typically take?

Installation time varies based on the system and property size. Most residential installations are completed within 1-2 days, while more complex commercial systems may take 3-5 days.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans to ensure your security system remains in optimal condition. Our plans include regular check-ups, software updates, and priority support.

Can I integrate different security systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in creating integrated security solutions where your CCTV, alarms, access control, and other systems work together seamlessly for maximum protection.

What brands of equipment do you use?

We work with industry-leading brands known for reliability and performance, including Hikvision, Dahua, Bosch, and more. All equipment comes with manufacturer warranties.

Do you provide remote monitoring services?

Yes, we offer remote monitoring solutions that allow you to access your security system from anywhere using a smartphone or computer. Our team can also provide professional monitoring services.
